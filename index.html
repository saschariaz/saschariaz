Sascha Riaz

Welcome! I am Postdoctoral Prize Research Fellow in Politics at Nuffield College (Oxford University).

Before coming to Oxford, I obtained a Ph.D in Government (2022) and Masters in Statistics (2020) from Harvard University.

Get in touch: sascha.riaz@nuffield.ox.ac.uk
